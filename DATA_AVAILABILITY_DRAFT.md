# Data Availability

Data generated in this study are available in Zenodo at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20679254 and in the associated GitHub repository at https://github.com/Levoder/gbd2023-masld-nafld-high-bmi-counterfactual.

The deposited record contains derived analytical tables, manuscript table files, figure source data, final figure files, WPP 2024 population-exposure review outputs, QC logs, file-level checksums, and analysis scripts. Public aggregate data reused in the analysis were obtained from the Global Burden of Disease 2023 Results Tool, the United Nations World Population Prospects 2024, and Natural Earth; these third-party sources remain available from their original providers under their respective access terms.

## Repository citation

Li R, Dong X, Wei X, et al. GBD 2023 MASLD/NAFLD-related liver burden counterfactual analysis, version v0.1.1-submission. Zenodo; 2026. doi:10.5281/zenodo.20679254.
